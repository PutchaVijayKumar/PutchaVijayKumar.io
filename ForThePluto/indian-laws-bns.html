The Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023


India consolidated some sectios of a few laws and renamed them. As of now, changes were made to the following acts.

  1. The Indian Penal Code, 1860
  2. The Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973
  3. The Indian Evidence Act, 1872

The below table shows the comparative changes in provisions of Indian Penal Code, 1860 and Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023.


Indian Penal Code, 1860 Bhartiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023
Section Heading Section Heading
1 Title and extent of operations of the code 1 Short title, commencement and application
2 Punishment of offences committed within India 1(3) Short title, commencement and application
3 Punishment of offences committed beyond, but which by law may be tried within, India 1(4) Short title, commencement and application
